Moravian Christmas Cookies

Source: Monika Grabkowska

Ingredients

1 pound brown sugar
1 pound butter
4 pounds flour
1 quart New Orleans cooking molasses
1/2 ounce ginger
1/2 cloves
1 teaspoon soda
1 orange, juice
1 teaspoon lemon zest

Directions

  1. Mix all together, except the flour, begin careful to hear enough to melt the butter
  2. Then " work in " the flour
  3. This makes a large quantity, as the cakes should be rolled very thin
  4. Keep the cookies in a dry place, and they will be crisp and delicious as long as they last, even for months
